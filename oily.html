Oily Skin Skincare Routine


Day:

1. Cleanse gently by washing your face with a gentle cleanser.
2. Hydrate by applying a hydrating toner or serum.
3. Moisturize with a rich moisturizer.
4. Protect your skin by applying sunscreen with SPF 30 or higher.
5. Optional: Apply eye cream for extra care.


Night:

1. Double cleanse using an oil-based cleanser to remove makeup, followed by a gentle cleanser.
2. Exfoliate 1-2 times a week to buff away dry patches with a gentle exfoliant.
3. Treat your skin by applying a hydrating serum.
4. Apply eye cream for nighttime care.
5. Moisturize with a nourishing night cream.
6. Optional: Apply face oil for an extra boost.
