Sensitive Skin Skincare Routine


Day:

1. Cleanse with a mild, fragrance-free cleanser.
2. Apply a soothing, hydrating moisturizer formulated for sensitive skin.
3. Use a sunscreen with physical blockers like zinc oxide or titanium dioxide to protect against UV rays without irritating the skin.
4. Consider applying a calming serum or cream to further soothe sensitivity.
5. Finish with a mineral-based makeup if desired, avoiding products with harsh chemicals or fragrances.


Night:

1. Gently cleanse the skin to remove impurities and makeup residue.
2. Apply a gentle, non-irritating exfoliant if needed, such as a chemical exfoliant with mild ingredients like lactic acid or a soft washcloth.
3. Then use hydrating, fragrance-free moisturizer to nourish and repair the skin overnight.
4. Consider using a hypoallergenic eye cream to moisturize and protect your eye area.
5. Optionally, apply a calming facial oil or cream.
