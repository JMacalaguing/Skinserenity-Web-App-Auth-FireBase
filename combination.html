Combination Skin Skincare Routine



Day:

1. Gently remove dirt, oil, and sweat with a foaming gel cleanser.
2. If needed, use a gentle exfoliating wash to remove dead skin cells, especially in the oily T-zone area.
3. Apply a lightweight serum with hyaluronic acid to hydrate and plump the skin. Look for formulas labeled as “non-comedogenic” to avoid clogging pores.
4. Apply a light, oil-free moisturizer to hydrate without clogging pores.
5. Protect your skin from sun damage with a broad-spectrum SPF 30 or higher sunscreen.


Night:

1. Remove makeup and impurities with a gentle cleanser.
2. Gently buff away dead skin cells with a chemical exfoliant containing AHAs or BHAs to treat oily areas and promote cell turnover.
3. Apply a targeted serum with retinol to address wrinkles and uneven skin tone.
4. Apply a richer moisturizer to hydrate dry areas of your face.
